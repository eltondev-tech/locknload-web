About Us

Welcome to Lock n' Load, where safety, skill, and responsibility come together in a premier indoor and outdoor shooting experience.

At Lock n' Load, we are proud to provide a modern, professionally managed shooting range designed to serve everyone — from first-time shooters and families to experienced marksmen and professionals. Our facility combines advanced indoor lanes with spacious outdoor ranges, giving our members the flexibility to train, practice, and improve in a controlled and supportive environment.

Safety is the foundation of everything we do. Our certified range officers, strict safety protocols, and full compliance with all local, state, and federal regulations ensure that every visit is secure, structured, and professionally supervised. We are committed to responsible firearm education and maintaining the highest operational standards.
